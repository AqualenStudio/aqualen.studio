Who is behind Aqualen

Aqualen Studio is a small label attached to Joytoart Gaming Limited (Cyprus). It focuses on water-heavy IP, systems design and long-form worldbuilding instead of pure “feature shopping”.

Joytoart Gaming Limited is the parent company handling publishing, finance and legal. Aqualen Studio is the creative / experimental label under it, where prototypes and new IPs are born.

We like tight loops, weird systems, and characters who feel like people instead of quest dispensers. We’re slowly building a connected “floodline” universe across multiple games and mediums.

Based in Cyprus & Chengdu, we work async across timezones, using UE, Unity, AI tooling and a lot of Notion boards.
