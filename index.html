About Aqualen Studio

Aqualen Studio is an independent game & worldbuilding practice led by a long-time action game designer with a soft spot for ocean ruins and weird myths. The studio works digitally between Cyprus and Chengdu, mixing Unreal Engine production with AI-assisted ideation and tooling.

The focus: build one strong diving-focused IP, then explore it through games, visual stories and experimental narrative formats.

Languages: EN / 中文 · Remote-friendly · Comfortable with long-term, slow-burn worldbuilding.

What we’re looking for

In the mid-term, the studio is interested in connecting with:

  • 3D environment artists comfortable with stylized ruins & deep-sea vibes;
  • Sound / music designers who enjoy quiet tension more than loud action;
  • Narrative collaborators into long-form worldbuilding and strange cities.

If that sounds like you, drop a line with a short intro and portfolio link.
