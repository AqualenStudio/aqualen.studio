Unnamed Diving Project

A first-person, open-water exploration game in a post-flooded world.

Current state

This project is in a pure exploration phase: prototypes, mood studies, mechanical sketches, and “what if” experiments inside Unreal Engine.

Once we lock the core loop and emotional spine, we’ll share more.

Goals

  • Make water feel like a character, not just a shader.
  • Blend salvage gameplay with quiet, reflective exploration.
  • Keep UI almost invisible and rely on world cues.
