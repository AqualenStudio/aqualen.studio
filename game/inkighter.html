Inkighter

A 2D ink-style combat experiment that helped define how we think about weight, impact, and rhythm in action games.

What it was

Inkighter was a long-running, self-funded project experimenting with brushstroke-driven combat, deliberate timing, and the feeling of painting your attacks onto the screen.

It never became the commercial product we wanted, but it remains a core part of our DNA and informs how we think about game feel.

What we learned

  • How to scope (and over-scope) a solo project.
  • How combat feel is more about timing & sound than raw animation.
  • The cost of building everything “from scratch” vs reusing tooling.

Status

  • Development: closed
  • Internal builds only
  • May reappear in future forms

Tech

  • Engine: Unity (URP)
  • Platform: PC (internal)
  • Art: ink-inspired 2D
